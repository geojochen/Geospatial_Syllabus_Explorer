About the Project

The Geospatial Syllabus Explorer is an open initiative to document and analyze the state of Geographic Information Science (GIS) education. By aggregating hundreds of syllabi from universities worldwide, I provide a window into what is being taught and how.

Whether you are a student looking for the right program, an instructor designing a new course, or a researcher tracking the evolution of the field, this tool offers unique insights into:

  • Software Trends: Adoption rates of ArcGIS, QGIS, Python, and R.
  • Skill Mapping: Where specialized topics like Lidar, UAS, and Web GIS are taught.
  • Pedagogy: The balance between project-based learning and traditional exams.
