Contribute to the Map

This project relies on the community to build a truly global picture of GIS education.
Whether you are an instructor or a department head, we welcome your syllabi.

Email Submission

Send your PDF or Word syllabus directly to the maintainer. I will parse the data and add it to the explorer.

GitHub Pull Request

If you prefer, you can submit a PR directly to our repository to add your syllabus to the collection.

Why contribute?

By sharing your syllabus, you help students find specialized programs and assist fellow educators in mapping software trends and learning outcomes. All contributions are credited to the originating institution and instructor.
